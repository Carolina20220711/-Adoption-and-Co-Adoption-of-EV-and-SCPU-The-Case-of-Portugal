The file UNOVA - Carolina de Oliveira - Vendas por distrito - 2011 a 2024.xlsx is not included in this repository due to GitHub's file size limitations.
To request this dataset, ask for:
Vehicle sales data broken down by:
- Year and month;
- Sales district/area;
- Energy type (e.g., BEV, GNC, etc.);
- Category (e.g., Ligeiro, Pesado, etc.);
- Type (e.g., Passageiros, Mercadorias
This information was provided upon request by ACAP and covers the years 2011 to 2024.
